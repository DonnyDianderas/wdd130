Lima

Lima, the capital of Peru, is a super vibrant city located on the Pacific coast. It has an amazing mix of history and modern culture, and it's also famous for its food, being the culinary capital of South America. In Lima, you can find everything from colonial buildings to skyscrapers, with iconic places like Plaza Mayor and ancient archaeological sites. The coast is full of beaches and stunning views, and you can't leave without trying a good ceviche. it's world-famous!
